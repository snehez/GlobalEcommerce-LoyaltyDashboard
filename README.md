## üöÄ Project Overview

This project presents a **comprehensive analysis of global e-commerce performance**, focusing on **repeat purchase behavior, product-level revenue drivers, plan profitability, discount effectiveness, and customer loyalty**.  

Using interactive dashboards with **cross-filtering, drill-down, and guided storytelling**, the report empowers business stakeholders to:

- Understand **what drives revenue**
- Identify **loyal and high-value customer segments**
- Evaluate **marketing campaign performance**
- Optimize **product pricing and subscription plans**
- Reduce churn and grow long-term profitability

---

## üß≠ Business Questions Addressed

| Business Question | Insight Outcome |
|------------------|----------------|
| How do total sales change by month? | Monthly trend analysis reveals seasonality and demand cycles. |
| Which channels bring in the most sales and loyal customers? | Sales + customer loyalty split across acquisition channels highlights ROI efficiency. |
| What percent of monthly sales comes from repeat buyers? | Repeat revenue ratio measures brand loyalty and retention strength. |
| Which products or plans generate the most revenue? | Product-level revenue breakdown identifies high-performing offerings. |
| Which products are most popular among loyal customers? | Loyalty-driven product adoption insights support personalized targeting. |
| How long do customers take to make their second purchase? | Purchase gap metrics reveal retention timing and remarketing opportunities. |
| Which discount codes are used most, and do they improve loyalty? | Discount effectiveness analysis shows whether promotions convert into repeat revenue. |
| What is the ASP (Average Selling Price) by country or currency? | Geographic pricing insights support global price strategy optimization. |
| Where do refunds occur most (product or channel)? | Refund hotspots highlight product quality issues or channel misalignment. |
| Do annual plans deliver higher revenue per customer than monthly plans? | Subscription cohort evaluation quantifies long-term value. |
| Which add-ons are frequently bought together (attach rate)? | Bundle optimization opportunities for increasing basket value. |

## üí° Key Insights

- **Repeat customers drive a disproportionately high share of total revenue**, confirming the need to strengthen loyalty efforts.
- **Certain sales channels generate high volume but weak loyalty**, suggesting inefficient acquisition spend.
- **Annual subscription plans deliver higher revenue per user**, validating incentives for plan upgrades.
- **Discount codes increase first-time conversions but do not guarantee repeat purchases**, indicating the need for follow-up engagement campaigns.
- **Refunds are concentrated in specific product segments**, highlighting potential product quality issues or expectation gaps.

## ‚úÖ Recommendations for Business Strategy

| Strategic Recommendation | Expected Outcome |
|-------------------------|----------------|
| Implement **personalized lifecycle email campaigns** post-purchase | Faster second-purchase conversion and stronger loyalty |
| Provide **bundle discounts / add-on upsell prompts** | Higher average order value & attach rate |
| Introduce or expand **loyalty / rewards programs** | Increased retention and customer lifetime value |
| Optimize **pricing for markets with lower ASP** | Improved competitiveness and conversion |
| Target channels with strong loyalty instead of only high volume | Higher marketing ROI |
